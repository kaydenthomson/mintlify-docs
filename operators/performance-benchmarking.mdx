## Solution overview

Compare locations and set performance goals.

**Outcome**: Data‑driven improvement and goal tracking.

## Features enabling this

- Benchmarks / Scorecards: Compare KPIs across units
- AI Assistant: Identify anomalies and trends
- Reports: Generate monthly performance reviews
- Forms / Feedback: Collect operator feedback

## Get started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Define KPIs">Create metrics for Revenue, COGS %, Labor %, EBITDA, etc.</Step>
  <Step title="Configure benchmarks">Choose which KPIs appear in benchmark comparisons.</Step>
  <Step title="Build a scorecard">Add a small set of metrics and the units to track.</Step>
  <Step title="Review & publish">Send a monthly report with highlights and action items.</Step>
</Steps>

## Best practices

- Keep to 3–6 KPIs per scorecard for clarity
- Exclude units without data for the selected period
- Use AI summaries to quickly highlight anomalies or wins
